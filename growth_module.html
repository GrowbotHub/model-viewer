Aeroponic Modules

We grow different plants: strawberries, lettuces, radishes, fennel, all chosen with regards to their facility of growth. The roots should be strong enough to resist all the displacements.

A pumping system sprays a nutrient solution every two minutes on the roots. For the lights, each module has a LED system at the bottom to shine light on the module underneath it.

Modules are powered via electrical connection on the shelves when the modules are stored.
