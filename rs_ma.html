Robotic Station

The robotic arm is equipped with a camera to check the growth stage of the plants. If it considers them ready, it grabs them to place them on a table accessible to the astronauts.

The robot can plant seeds and pollinate flowers. It is also tasked with moving plants around the different Aeroponic Modules to make sure they all have enough space to grow.

Mixing Area

The Mixing Area aims to refill the aeroponic modules according to the plants' need.

It is composed of tanks containing various solutions such as fertilizer, pH regulators, nutrient supplement and water. Each tank is connected to a pump, able to produce the exact volume of liquid necessary to obtain a solution matching the need of the plants.
