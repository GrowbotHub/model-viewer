The Full Robot

Say hi to GrowBotHub!

This robot is designed to grow plants in a self-sustained, closed loop fashion. It is composed of several subsystems, all communicating with each other through the robot's own Local Area Network.

The plants are stored in aeroponic modules, which sit in a metallic shelving structure. There are four shelves with four units. Each unit can hold 300 kg.

There a spaced by around 30cm in order to be able to store the plants during at least half of their growth. When they get higher, one module is removed to give additional space.
